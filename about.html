About Us


InterviewPrep is a web-based platform that provides job seekers the tools, and tips to prepare them for their interviews.
InterviewPrep has provided job seekers with a virtual interview simulation where they can practice their interview skills with a simulated employer.
InterviewPrep would also like to hear any feedbacks you have on our simulator.
InterviewPrep is taking the global approach to internationalize their company by providing a multi-language feature for international job seekers, for both the simulator and the interview tips.
